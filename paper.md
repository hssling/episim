# Summary

EPISIM is a Python simulation lab for deterministic, auditable demonstrations of
epidemiological and allied-science study designs. It exposes a common `Study`
archive contract, a registry of runnable designs, notebooks for teaching and
methods demonstration, and deployment surfaces for GitHub, Hugging Face Spaces,
Kaggle, Docker, and Zenodo.

# Statement of need

Researchers and trainees often learn study designs as disconnected examples.
EPISIM provides a single reproducible platform where observational studies,
randomized trials, quasi-experiments, transmission models, decision models, and
mixed-methods simulations share the same archive and reporting contract.
